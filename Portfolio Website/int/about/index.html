About Ginny Graybill Designs

New Beginnings, Strong Roots

I'm a San Diego based designer, but a Mainer at heart. I moved out to San Diego in 2019 to pursue an education just months before everything we knew about the world changed. Through the act of moving 3000 miles away from home, and focusing on my formal education while the world fell apart around me I learned a lot about myself and what I'm capable of withstanding.

I'm not afraid to take the big leaps to achieve the vision I see for myself, and I'm not afraid to speak my truths. I can make worthy pieces of design quickly. My first passion was art, and my newest is design. I find it fascinating how intertwined they can be. The middle of the venn diagram of art and design is where I want to live my creative career.

I want to make websites that look beautiful from the smallest mobile screens to the largest desktop monitors. I want to make intuitive designs, ones that don't keep the user guessing. I want to use my design experiences to enhance the user's experiences. I want to create websites and designs that stand the test of time.

When I'm not working on school projects, I explore other creative pursuits such as drawing, making silly tiktoks and looking for the next thing to hyperfocus my creative energy on. If you like what I do, please feel free to reach out through social media, or the contact form on this site.
