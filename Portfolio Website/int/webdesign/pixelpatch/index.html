Web Design The Pixel Patch

The Pixel Patch

A cozy, clicker style game where the player can grow their own little digital flower patch. Earn coins by growing and selling the flowers and by tending to them with mouse clicks. When a flower is clicked, it grows a little quicker. The longer your play, the faster your flowers grow when they're clicked.

Coins, time played and total clicks are stored useing local data. This game was built using javascript/jQuery and DOM manipulation.

Best viewed on laptop and desktop screen sizes. A small sound warning: while the sounds are calming, they aren't able to be disabled.
